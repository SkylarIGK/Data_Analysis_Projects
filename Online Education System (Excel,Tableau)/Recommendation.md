## Online Learning Environment & Infrastructure
1. Improve Internet Accessibility for Rural Students:
- Insight: Students from rural areas report lower internet facility scores compared to urban students.
- Recommendation: Educational institutions and policymakers should improve internet infrastructure in rural areas and provide subsidized data plans or offline learning resources to ensure equitable access to online education.



## Academic Performance & Study Habits
2. Encourage Balanced Study and Sleep Routines:
- Insight: Students with moderate study hours and adequate sleep (5–8 hours) show better online performance.
- Recommendation: Institutions should provide study planning guidance and wellness awareness programs to promote healthy study–sleep balance among students.



3. Limit Excessive Social Media Usage:
- Insight: Higher social media usage is associated with lower academic performance and satisfaction.
- Recommendation: Students should be encouraged to manage screen time effectively through time-management workshops and digital well-being initiatives.



## Learning Support & Engagement
4. Strengthen Faculty Interaction and Doubt-Clearing Sessions:
- Insight: Higher interaction and better doubt-clearing scores are strongly linked to improved performance and satisfaction.
- Recommendation: Instructors should conduct regular live sessions, interactive discussions, and timely doubt-clearing activities to enhance student engagement.



5. Support Group Studies and Dedicated Study Spaces:
- Insight: Students engaged in group studies and those with separate study rooms perform better in online learning.
- Recommendation: Institutions should promote collaborative learning activities, and families should support students by providing a quiet, dedicated study environment where possible.


## Student Satisfaction & Learning Outcomes
6. Provide Additional Support for Younger Students:
- Insight: Performance and satisfaction levels vary across age groups, with younger students facing more challenges in online learning.
- Recommendation: Targeted mentoring, structured learning schedules, and additional academic support should be provided to younger learners to improve their online learning experience.
